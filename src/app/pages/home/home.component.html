Meet the Sharks

Barcelona’s Ultimate Frisbee club

About Us

We are a team based in Barcelona that plays and promotes Ultimate Frisbee on the beach and grass. We welcome players of all levels! We play and train across three divisions: Mixed, Open, and Women.

🏆 Tournaments

We organize BBU and compete nationally and internationally.

💪 Trainings

Weekly sessions open to new and experienced players alike.

🌍 Community

We value fair play, teamwork, and the spirit of the game.
